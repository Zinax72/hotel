    Our Amenities

    Pool & Recreation

    JABUBU Hotel features a stunning infinity pool with panoramic views, open daily from 6am to 10pm. Guests can unwind at our poolside lounge with complimentary towel service and refreshments.

    Fitness & Wellness

    Our fully equipped 24-hour fitness center offers state-of-the-art equipment, personal training services, and a dedicated yoga studio. The in-house spa provides a range of massage and beauty treatments.

    Dining

    Enjoy breakfast, lunch, and dinner at our signature restaurant, offering both local and international cuisine. The rooftop bar serves handcrafted cocktails and light bites throughout the evening.

    Room Amenities

    Every room is equipped with high-speed Wi-Fi, a 50" Smart TV with streaming services, individual air conditioning, a private bathroom with rainfall shower, mini bar, and an in-room digital safe.

    Services

    We offer complimentary airport shuttle transfers, secure on-site parking, 24-hour front desk assistance, and daily housekeeping. Our concierge team is always available to arrange tours and activities.
